Float-Based Layout Example.

Main Article

Welcome to our digital magazine! This article demonstrates how text can wrap neatly around images using the float property. The image floats to the left while text flows beside it — just like in printed magazines and newspapers.

Floats were the first way to create multi-column layouts before Flexbox and Grid existed. Here, we use float: left and float: right to position sections. The column-count property helps split text into columns, giving a real magazine-like look.

On smaller screens, media queries make this layout responsive — columns collapse, floats are removed, and the page becomes a single readable column. Resize your browser to see it change!
